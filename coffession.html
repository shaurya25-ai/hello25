There’s Something I Want to Tell You...

I don’t know when it happened… maybe slowly, maybe all at once…

But somewhere between our conversations, your laughter, your kindness, and the comfort I feel around you…

Something changed inside me.

You became someone I look for. Someone I think about. Someone whose presence feels like home.

And after keeping this in for a long time… I just want to say it —

I love you. In a way that’s deeper than friendship.

I don’t expect anything from you… I just wanted you to know what my heart has been feeling.
