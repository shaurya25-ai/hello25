Before You Came...

Before you came into my life, everything felt quiet… almost too quiet. Days looked the same, nights felt longer, and even in a room full of people, I felt alone.

Life was simple, but it was colourless — like a sky without sunrise, like music playing without emotion, like a story still waiting for its first beautiful chapter.

And then you came. Slowly, softly… without even trying.

You brought colours I never knew I was missing. Warmth I didn’t know I needed. And a kind of peace that feels rare in this world.

My life didn’t suddenly become perfect — but it became **alive**. And that changed everything.
